# CodChat

A collaborative coding platform where you can work with your team, chat, and get AI help instantly.

## Features

- **Team Collaboration**  
  Invite team members and work together in real-time on your projects.

- **Integrated Chat**  
  Chat with your team members directly within the platform while coding.

- **AI Assistant**  
  Type `@ai` before your message to get AI help for your queries.
